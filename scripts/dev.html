Prerequisites

To use an email address for sending, 3 step are required.

1. Verify mailbox permissions/ownership with seLabs

2. Verify mailbox permissions/ownership with Amazon SES

3. Update the DNS records for your email with your domain provider - this gets your emails out of spam


For each of these steps you will receive an email with details instructions.

Click Verify to begin the process. And again each time you have completed a step.

These steps need to be completed only once per email address.

Personalisation Variables


Correo allows you to personalise your email content.

To do so, you must specify the variable in your subject and/or html and you must a provide a value for the variable in your csv.

To specify the variable in the subject line or html, place it between double curly brackets, e.g. {{variable}}

To provide a value for it in the csv, create a column with a matching name, but no brackets, e.g. variable

Any non-alphanumeric characters will be ignored. e.g. {{Var.Name}} is the same as {{VarName}}.

If your variables do not match, your submission will be rejected.

Example 1: No variables

Subject:

Need car insurance?

CSV:
email
jdoe@company2.co.uk
johnsmith@company1.com
HTML:

<p>Hi there</p>

<p>Are you interested in insurance?</p>

<p>Kind regards</p>

Example 2: Some variables

Subject:

Need car insurance?

CSV:
email name
jdoe@company2.co.uk Jane
johnsmith@company1.com John
HTML:

<p>Hi {{name}}</p>

<p>Are you interested in insurance?</p>

<p>Kind regards</p>

Example 3: Variables everywhere

Subject:

Need insurance for your {{car}}?

CSV:
email name car
jdoe@company2.co.uk Jane Mazda
johnsmith@company1.com John Ford
HTML:

<p>Hi {{name}}</p>

<p>Are you interested in insurance for your {{car}}?</p>

<p>Kind regards</p>

Example 4: Failure - mismatched variables

Subject:

Need insurance?

CSV:
email person
jdoe@company2.co.uk Jane
johnsmith@company1.com John
HTML:

<p>Hi {{name}}</p>

<p>Are you interested in insurance?</p>

<p>Kind regards</p>

Example 5: Failure - missing variables

Subject:

Need insurance?

CSV:
email name
jdoe@company2.co.uk Jane
johnsmith@company1.com John
HTML:

<p>Hi there</p>

<p>Are you interested in insurance?</p>

<p>Kind regards</p>
