Personalisation Variables


Correo allows you to personalise your email content (i.e. each recipient can receive different email body text).

To do so, you must specify dynamic text variables in your email subject and/or in your HTML email body.

At send time, the values of the dynamic variables for each email recipient are looked up in your uploaded CSV file.

Therefore, the names and formatting of the dynamic variables must perfectly match between CSV, HTML and email subject.

Failure to do so will result in your email failing to send (although we do have guardrails in place to pick up obvious errors).

To specify a dynamic variable in your subject line or HTML email body, place the variable name between double braces (curly brackets), e.g. {{recipient_name}}

To specify a dynamic variable in your CSV, create a column with the variable name with no double braces, e.g. recipient_name



Example 1: No variables

Subject:

Need car insurance?

CSV:
email
jdoe@company2.co.uk
johnsmith@company1.com
HTML:

<p>Hi there</p>

<p>Are you interested in insurance?</p>

<p>Kind regards</p>

Example 2: Single variable

Subject:

Need car insurance?

CSV:
email name
jdoe@company2.co.uk Jane
johnsmith@company1.com John
HTML:

<p>Hi {{name}}</p>

<p>Are you interested in insurance?</p>

<p>Kind regards</p>

Note:

It is also acceptable to have a variable in the subject line while omitting it from the HTML body.

Example 3: Multiple variables

Subject:

Need insurance for your {{tag}} {{car}}?

CSV:
email name car tag
jdoe@company2.co.uk Jane Mazda zippy
johnsmith@company1.com John Ford flashy
HTML:

<p>Hi {{name}}</p>

<p>Are you interested in insurance for your {{car}}?</p>

<p>Kind regards</p>

Example 4: Failure - mismatched variables

Subject:

Need insurance?

CSV:
email person
jdoe@company2.co.uk Jane
johnsmith@company1.com John
HTML:

<p>Hi {{name}}</p>

<p>Are you interested in insurance?</p>

<p>Kind regards</p>

Example 5: Failure - missing variables

Subject:

Need insurance?

CSV:
email name
jdoe@company2.co.uk Jane
johnsmith@company1.com John
HTML:

<p>Hi there</p>

<p>Are you interested in insurance?</p>

<p>Kind regards</p>

Note:

The submission will also be rejected if the HTML and/or subject line contain a variable but the CSV does not.
