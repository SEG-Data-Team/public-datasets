Prerequisites

To use an email address for sending, 3 step are required.

1. Verify mailbox permissions/ownership with seLabs

2. Verify mailbox permissions/ownership with Amazon SES

3. Update the DNS records for your email with your domain provider - this gets your emails out of spam


For each of these steps you will receive an email with details instructions.

Click Verify to begin the process. And again each time you have completed a step.

These steps need to be completed only once per email address.
